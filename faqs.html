Certifyd Creatives is a design agency aiming to offer affordable and budget friendly design services to the businesses and enterprises with no limits meaning you can order unlimited designs and revisions by subscribing to our unlimited graphic design service.
Our headquarters is in Georgia, USA. We also have dedicated teams that work in different time zones; we promise effective communication and collaboration processes and we ensure that our client’s order requests are honored and accomplished timely and so their inquires and support tickets are responded quickly.
We have highly experienced yet creative team of designers who hold strong expertise into creative Graphic designing, UI/UX design, Web design, Social Media design, Marketing & Advertisement collateral design, Packaging design and all that a business or an enterprise needs. Please visit ‘Our Services’ to check more about our design expertise and offering.
Our pricing starts from $99 monthly. We charge no set-up fees and no cancellations charges. Please visit ‘Our Prices’ for more details about monthly subscription plans.
It’s quite simple, select our unlimited design package of your choice and place your order. Upon successful order, you get a dashboard to manage all your design orders and account settings.
To start with we need your requirements for the design, what design you need, desired size, the text or content that you want to have on the design, your logo, color theme or any other details that you wish to see in your design. It will also be good if you can share some of your past work, samples, or references that you like.
The foremost fact about our process is that we don’t outsource any of the work and we are focused on serving our clients with fully custom and rich designs. Our team are professionals that abide by internal guidelines and confidential polices. We have professionally channelized communication and execution process with complete transparency so even if at any point of time your dedicated account manager is unavailable, another manager can take over and move forward.
Once you place the order, you get a secured dashboard to manage your orders and other associated account functions. At the same time you get assigned a dedicated account manager who communicate with you on your all your design orders and then collaborates with our creative project managers to take your design orders forward in to the designing phase with creative design teams who creatively & timely complete your design orders.
We process the design orders within 24 hours and within 48 from placing any design order you get the 2-3 design concepts for review. If you need any revision in your chosen design then again the same process is repeated and once you approve the design we send the complete design package for you to download.
You can submit the design request primarily using the project management and collaboration system which you get access after Signing up with us. You can track the complete progress on your design order and can also submit the design request from here too.
We understand your urgency and we can surely assist you with urgency twice in a month, so when you place the design order you need to mention and select the check box ‘Urgent design request’ and we process and present you the design within 12 – 24 business hours
